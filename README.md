🌊 Heavy Metal Pollution Index (HMPI) Calculator

A web-based application developed during Smart India Hackathon (SIH) 2025 to automate the calculation of the Heavy Metal Pollution Index (HMPI) using the Mohan Atal Method. The platform analyzes heavy metal concentrations in water samples and determines water quality status through standardized environmental assessment techniques.

🚀 Overview

The HMPI Calculator helps researchers, environmental agencies, and policymakers assess groundwater and drinking water quality by calculating pollution indices from heavy metal concentration data. Users can input water sample values for metals such as Lead (Pb), Mercury (Hg), Cadmium (Cd), Chromium (Cr), and Arsenic (As), and the system automatically computes the HMPI value and categorizes water quality.

✨ Features

* 📊 Automated HMPI calculation using the Mohan Atal Method
* 💧 Water quality assessment based on heavy metal concentrations
* 📈 Interactive dashboard with visual reports and charts
* 📍 Support for geo-coordinated water sample analysis
* ⚡ Real-time pollution status evaluation
* 🛡️ Reduced human error compared to manual calculations

🎯 Problem Statement

Manual computation of Heavy Metal Pollution Indices is time-consuming, error-prone, and difficult to scale. This project automates the entire process, providing fast, reliable, and consistent pollution assessments for environmental monitoring and public health protection.

🌍 Impact

* Supports data-driven environmental decision-making
* Helps identify contaminated water sources

📚 Methodology

The system processes heavy metal concentration data collected from water samples and applies the Mohan Atal HMPI methodology to calculate pollution indices. The resulting HMPI value is compared against standard water quality thresholds to determine whether the water is safe, moderately polluted, or highly contaminated.
